Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Pulse JK ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Pulse JK mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Pulse JK is designed to be a news aggregation service focused on providing publicly available news and updates from Jammu & Kashmir. We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users, including but not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your precise location data
  • Your device identifiers (like advertising IDs)
  • Your unique user IDs
  • Any personal content you view or interact with within the app (e.g., reading history)

Information Processed by Third Parties

While Pulse JK does not collect or store personal data, the App uses third-party services for functionality such as displaying advertisements. These third parties may collect certain information to serve personalized ads and measure performance. Data collected by third parties is governed by their own privacy policies, and Pulse JK does not have control over this data.

External Browser/Email Launching (url_launcher)

When you tap on a news article or email within the App, we use the url_launcher library to open the content in your device's default browser or email client. Your activity is governed by the privacy policies of those services. Pulse JK does not collect data from these sessions.

App Version Information (package_info_plus)

The "About" screen displays the App's version number. This pertains to the App itself, not your personal identity.

How We Use Information

Since Pulse JK does not collect personal data, we do not use personal data for any purpose. Public news content is fetched solely to display it within the App.

Data Security

As no personal data is collected or stored, there is no personal data to secure. We are committed to maintaining app security and delivering public content reliably.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App or on our website. You are advised to review the Privacy Policy periodically.
